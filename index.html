Meet your therapist

Meet Weronika

Hi, I'm Weronika — a warm, approachable therapist here to help you navigate life's ups and downs. I specialise in anxiety, autism, trauma, and relationship challenges, using my background in Psychology and a Master's in Integrative Psychotherapy and Counselling to support you in a way that feels right for you.

I know that everyone's journey is different, so I don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. Instead, I draw from a range of therapies — psychodynamic, humanistic, cognitive behavioural therapy, and internal family systems therapy — to create sessions that meet your needs. My goal is to provide a safe and supportive space where you can explore your thoughts, heal from past experiences, and develop the tools to move forward with confidence.

How I can help

Therapy should feel like a conversation with someone who genuinely understands and cares. In our sessions, we'll work together to build resilience and find practical ways to handle life's challenges. Whether you're struggling with depression, anxiety, PTSD, or low self-esteem, I'm here to help you make sense of it all. As a registered member of the BACP, I ensure a professional and ethical practice so you can feel safe and supported every step of the way.

Frequently asked questions

If your question is not listed below or if you need further clarification or assistance in scheduling a consultation, please complete the contact form at the bottom of the page. We'll be happy to assist you.

How long does a therapy session last?

I offer 60-minute and 90-minute sessions. You can choose the session length that best suits your needs. Scheduling can be arranged via email when you reach out or after your consultation.

What types of therapy do you offer?

I offer therapy for those dealing with depression, anxiety, PTSD, childhood trauma, ADHD, autism, self-esteem, and relationship issues. My approach integrates psychodynamic, humanistic, CBT, psychoanalytic, inner-child, and internal family systems therapy, tailored to individual needs.

How do I book a session?

To schedule an online session or a consultation, please fill out the contact form below. I will respond within 24 hours to discuss your needs and arrange a suitable appointment at your convenience.

What can I expect in my first therapy session?

In your first session, we'll discuss your concerns, goals, and what support you need. Together, we'll create an action plan, with your input guiding the sessions while I provide professional insight to maximise impact in the time we have together.
